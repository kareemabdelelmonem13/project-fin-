THE AIM OF THE PROJECT

This project is intended to facilitate the clearance procedures for graduates. As it is a project aimed at helping graduates This idea was proposed due to the increasing number of graduates and the difficulty of the procedures followed to clear the student, and from here this idea came, including saving time and effort, as through this idea it does not require the student to go to the graduate administration or to go to many agencies. This idea also discusses the problem of crowding and avoids the many problems and complaints from students or from the concerned departments.
